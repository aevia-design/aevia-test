# Aevia — Project Brief

## What we're building
A premium photo book service based in Vienna. 
Concierge model — customer uploads photos, we design and print, 
customer approves before paying.

## The page we're replicating and improving
Current page: https://aevia-v1.webflow.io/templates/vows
This is a product page for one photo book template called "Vows".

## What the page needs to do
- Show the product (photo book images)
- Explain what's included (template style, pages, price)
- Allow page count selection (40 or 80 pages)
- Show optional add-ons
- CTA: "Create your book" → goes to order form

## Brand direction
- Premium, editorial, art-forward
- NOT generic photo book (not Shutterfly, not Photobox)
- Reference: Artifact Uprising product page style
- Typography: serif, generous whitespace, minimal
- Colors: off-white background, near-black text, warm accents

## What's wrong with current version
- Layout feels basic
- Add-on images are placeholders
- Third thumbnail slot is empty
- No visual hierarchy between sections

## Competitor reference (product page only)
https://www.artifactuprising.com/photo-books/hardcover-travel-album
Note: They have more product variants than us. 
Focus on their layout, typography, whitespace — not their feature set.

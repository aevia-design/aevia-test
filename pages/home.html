Process

Your memories, beautifully bound
— without the effort

Four steps. No design skills required.

1
Choose a template
Pick the occasion and page count that fits your story. Every template is designed in-house.
2
Upload your photos
Share via Google Drive, Dropbox, or direct upload. Add notes, captions, dates — as much or as little as you like.
3
We design it
Our team composes each spread by hand. You receive a full PDF preview to review and request any changes.
4
Approve & receive
Happy with the design? We print and ship. No payment until you approve — guaranteed.
Why Aevia

The Aevia touch

We believe a photo book should feel like something worth passing down.

Done for you
You supply the memories. We handle every design decision — layout, spacing, typesetting, sequence.
Tactile & lasting
170gsm matte art paper. Linen hardcover. Sewn-bound signatures. Built to be kept for decades.
Curated templates
Every layout is designed for its occasion — not adapted from a generic grid. Proportions, pacing, and whitespace are all considered.
Produced responsibly

What do printed photos actually look like?

Printed photos will always look different from your phone screen — and that's not a flaw, it's physics. Screens emit light; paper reflects it. Colors will be slightly less vivid, and very dark or backlit photos may print darker than expected. A good rule of thumb: reduce your screen brightness to 50% to approximate how a photo will look in print.

For best results we recommend photos taken in good light, with the main subject filling most of the frame. Small faces in group shots, distant landscapes, or heavily filtered photos may lose sharpness or detail in print.

What quality should my photos be?

Your phone camera is more than good enough — modern smartphones shoot at resolutions well above what printing requires. The main things to avoid: heavy Instagram filters, extreme crops, and very dark or strongly backlit shots. If you've edited your photos in an app, that's fine — just avoid over-sharpening or heavy color grading.

We print in sRGB color profile. Photos taken on a smartphone are automatically in sRGB. If you're uploading photos from a DSLR camera, check with your photographer that the color profile is set correctly.

For best quality and correct photo ordering, upload directly from your phone or transfer via USB cable / AirDrop. Avoid WhatsApp or email — they reduce quality and remove date information.

How many photos do I need?

Depending on the template, you'll need between 50 and 80 photos. We'll let you know the exact range once you choose your template and page count.

How does ordering work?

Aevia is currently in early access. Here's how the process works: choose your occasion and template, upload your photos via our order form, and we'll assemble your book within 48 hours. You'll receive two layout options to review — choose your favourite, then complete payment. Your book goes to print immediately after.

How do I pay?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe — a secure, industry-standard payment processor. You are only charged after you approve your book layout, not upfront.

Do you deliver outside Austria?

Yes — we currently deliver across the EU. Delivery times for other EU countries are typically 5–7 business days from dispatch.

# Plan 10-01 — bloom.html: Functional Page Selector + Photo Count

## Objective
Add a "Personalise your book" section to bloom.html above the order CTA. Five FP options with spread preview images (placeholder photos from existing bloom.html carousel) and descriptions. Photo count calculator shows customers exactly how many photos to prepare based on selections + page count.

## Context
- @pages/bloom.html — existing product page (carousel photos = use these as FP preview placeholders)
- @assets/Template_Toddler/template-data.js — slot counts per spread type
- @.planning/BRIEF.md — FP descriptions

## FP descriptions for customers

| FP | What customer provides | Preview placeholder |
|----|----------------------|---------------------|
| Birthday wishes | A birthday message (text) + 1 special photo | bloom carousel photo |
| First words | Child's first words / funny phrases (text) | bloom carousel photo |
| First steps | 1 special photo + short note (e.g. date of first steps) | bloom carousel photo |
| Favourite toy | 1 special photo of the favourite toy | bloom carousel photo |
| Art gallery | 2 images of child's artwork + short descriptions | bloom carousel photo |

## Photo count formula
Regular photos needed = sum of regular photo slots across the book sequence.
Special photos = 1 per FP (birthday heart, toy), 2 for art gallery, 0 for text-only FPs.

Slot counts per spread type (regular photos only):
- SP0: 1 | SP1: 3 | SP2: 3 | SP3: 3 | SP4: 3 | SP5: 3 | SP6: 2
- FP1 Birthday: 0 regular (replaces standard spread, left=text, right=special)
- FP2 Words: 1 regular (right page) + 0 special (left=text)
- FP3 Toy: 1 regular (right page) + 1 special (left)
- FP4 Steps: 1 regular (right page) + 1 special (left)
- FP5 Art: 0 regular + 2 special artwork

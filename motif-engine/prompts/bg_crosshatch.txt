kevinlucbert_style. Full-page background texture for a premium photobook interior page.

An all-over abstract pen hatching field filling the entire canvas edge to edge. No recognizable objects. Dense directional biro pen lines covering the whole surface — areas of parallel marks in different directions, creating a shifting textural landscape. Lines are slightly curved, organic, never mechanical. Some zones denser, some lighter, with breathing room between sections.

Color: two to three vibrant flat color zones — warm orange-coral, deep blue-purple, and a hint of bright green or ochre. Each color zone has its own distinct line direction. Colors are bold and saturated but the overall composition stays calm and even — no single corner dominates.

No objects, no shapes, no figures, no text, no borders, no vignette. Pure mark-making across the full frame. The texture should feel like an abstract landscape reduced to its essence — rich but quiet. Photos placed on top will remain the visual heroes.

White paper shows through between lines — the background breathes.
